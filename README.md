## About Me

I’m a software engineer from Portugal focused on building practical, reliable, and well-architected systems.

I recently completed my **BSc in Computing and Informatics Engineering at FEUP** and will be starting an **MSc in Computer Science and Engineering at DTU** in August 2026.

My work sits between **full-stack product development**, **backend systems**, **distributed / IoT architectures**, and increasingly **efficient AI systems**. I enjoy projects where software needs to be more than just functional: it has to be maintainable, performant, and useful in the real world.

I’m also the founder of **Ocean Informatix**, where I build websites, platforms, automations, and custom software for clients.
